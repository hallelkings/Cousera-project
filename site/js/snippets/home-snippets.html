About Him

Hallel Kings is a Nigerian DJ, Music Producer, Web Developer and Artiste. He is versatile when it comes to Music production, which ranges from EDM to Pop to Afropop etc. His tracks are usually identified with emotional melodies usually made with the guitar or piano, flowing rythm, and a bit of African vibe to some of his songs. The songs uploaded on his Social Media Accounts/Pages also includes Remixes, Mashups and Collabs.

How He Started

Before Hallel Kings became an Official Artiste, he started as a beatmaker at the age of sixteen (an inexperienced one at that, lol). He was a big fan of Electronic House songs and EDM and his biggest role model was Avicii, who inspired him alot into dabbling with electronic sounds.
